UPGRADED

Ana, an ambitious intern in the art world, impresses her boss Claire by catching an error at an auction. However, Claire's assistants sabotage Ana's trip to London. Ana's encounter with a wealthy man, William, leads to a series of misunderstandings where Ana pretends to be an art director. Despite the lies, Ana and William grow close. Ana's deception is revealed, leading to her firing, but she earns the respect of William's mother Catherine. Ana's honesty leads to a successful auction, and she rekindles her relationship with William after he arrives unexpectedly at her gallery opening.
