FALIMY

Falimy is a 2023 Indian Malayalam-language comedy-drama film written and directed by Nithish Sahadev in his directorial debut. The film stars Basil Joseph, Jagadish and Manju Pillai. It narrates the journey of a dysfunctional middle-class family to Varanasi and the realisations they gain from it. Family of self-centered individuals sets out on a journey to fulfill their grandfather's pilgrimage wish, partly to prevent his frequent escapes and partly to find a distraction from their personal problems.
